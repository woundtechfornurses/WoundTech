Assessment

"Use finger tips to pull at skin around wound and observe."

"Nurse lightly pushes legs outward."

Removal

"Nurse cuts away and unwraps dressing on left foot."

Cleaning

"In round motion, wipe gauze over heel. Then wipe back and forth to clean."

Treatment
